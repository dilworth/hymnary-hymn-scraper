Title
Go Down, Moses
The United Methodist Hymnal #448

Verse 1
When Israel was in Egypt's land
let my people go

oppressed so hard they could not stand
let my people go


Chorus
Refrain
Go down, Moses

way down in Egypt's land
tell old Pharaoh

to let my people go


Verse 2
"Thus saith the Lord," bold Moses said
let my people go

"if not, I'll smite your firstborn dead,"
let my people go. (Refrain)


Verse 3
No more shall they in bondage toil
let my people go

let them come out with Egypt's spoil
let my people go. (Refrain)


Verse 4
We need not always weep and mourn
let my people go

and wear those slavery chains forlorn
let my people go. (Refrain)


Verse 5
Come, Moses, you will not get lost
let my people go

stretch out your rod and come across
let my people go. (Refrain)


Verse 6
As Israel stood by the water's side
let my people go

at God's command it did divide
let my people go. (Refrain)


Verse 7
When they had reached the other shore
let my people go

they sang a song of triumph o'er
let my people go. (Refrain)


Verse 8
O Moses, the cloud shall cleave the way
let my people go

a fire by night, a shade by day
let my people go. (Refrain)


Verse 9
Your foes shall not before you stand
let my people go

and you'll possess fair Canaan's land
let my people go. (Refrain)


Verse 10
This world's a wilderness of woe
let my people go

O let us on to Canaan go
let my people go. (Refrain)


Verse 11
O let us all from bondage flee
let my people go

and let us all in Christ be free
let my people go. (Refrain)
