Title
Who Is He in Yonder Stall
The United Methodist Hymnal #190

Verse 1
Who is he in yonder stall
at whose feet the shepherds fall


Chorus
Refrain
'Tis the Lord, O wondrous story

'Tis the Lord, the King of glory
at his feet we humbly fall

crown him, crown him Lord of all


Verse 2
Lo, at midnight who is he
prays in dark Gethsemane? [Refrain]


Verse 3
Who is he in Calvary's throes
asks for blessings on his foes? [Refrain]


Verse 4
Who is he that from the grave
comes to heal and help and save? [Refrain]


Verse 5
Who is he that from yon throne
rules the world of light alone? [Refrain]
