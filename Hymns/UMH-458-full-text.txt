Title
Dear Lord, for All in Pain
The United Methodist Hymnal #458

Verse 1
Dear Lord, for all in pain
we pray to thee

O come and smite again
thine enemy


Verse 2
Give to thy servants skill
to soothe and bless

and to the tired and ill
give quietness


Verse 3
And, Lord, to those who know
pain may not cease

come near, that even so
they may have peace

Blank
